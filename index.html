A Little About Me

I am foremost a military brat; having grown up throughout the midwest. Born in Columbia, Missouri on May 11, 1992, the world was never to be quite the same after that date. Not that I have radically changed the world, but I truly feel the world changes everyday in meaningful ways. My family consists of a school nurse mother and a air force psychologist father, a speech pathologist older sister and a younger sister. I graduated in 2014 from the University of Missouri-Columbia with a bachelor's in biology and have worked various jobs within laboratory environments. I work as an independent research analyst and am looking for more work within biotechnology. Data Science is a passion, and I hope make more meaning with it in more imaginable ways.
